Seafood Marinara Pasta

It's all about the freshest blend of your favourite seafood with tomato & basil sauce. Your squid rings, shrimp, clams and mussels are cooked to perfection! Get a rush of the Mediterranean flavour with every single twirl of pasta!

  • RECIPE

🔪INGREDIENTS

500 g squid rings
500 g shrimp
500 g clams
500 g mussels
400 g tomatoes
4-5 miced garlic
2 tbsp hot sauce

5 tbsp olive oil
1 bottle tomato & basil sauce
550 g spaghetti
Chopped Parsley
Salt & pepper
Water
Rock salt

🍲STEPS

Prepare ingredients before cooking (mis en place)
Saute garlic with olive oil in a large saucepan for a minute. Stir in tomatoes. Add squid rings and continue stirring. After a few minutes, press down tomatoes to release the juice.
Once squid rings are soft, add the rest of the seafood. Cover for two minutes to ensure they cook through. Stir in the hot sauce, parsley, salt and pepper. Cover again and wait for the shells to open.
Time to add the tomato & basil sauce! Taste as you season. Cover the pan and cook for 10 minutes. Serve with spaghetti (follow instructions according to the package). Enjoy!💗 (www.vincenzosplate.com)
